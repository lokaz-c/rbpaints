About us

R&B paint's story

R&B Paints, established in 2022 and based in Kigali, Rwanda, is a paint manufacturing business committed to providing fine quality paints that are accessible and user-friendly for both professional painters and DIY enthusiasts.

Our mission is to become a leading paint manufacturer in the region, promoting ethical and professional business practices. We focus on delivering high-quality coating solutions while being environmentally conscious and attentive to individual preferences.

Our vision is to build a reputable paint manufacturing industry known for its integrity, meticulous craftsmanship, and commitment to customer satisfaction, embodying our core values of integrity, ingenuity, accessibility, respect, and being customer-oriented.

R&B Paints empowers professional painters, DIY enthusiasts, and creative individuals to bring their artistic visions to life with high-quality paint products. Our paints enhance business environments and personal homes alike, adding beauty and durability. Our dedicated community continues to grow, fostering creativity and excellence in every brushstroke.
